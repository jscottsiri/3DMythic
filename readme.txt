To begin this game, click begin.

You move by clicking to a space. You may move a max of 8 tiles in a turn and moving will end your character's turn.
You have 2 characters, if they run out of health you lose.

Complete your objective to win.

Your characters are off-white. Enemies are red. Cyril is dark red.

Before moving, you can take certain actions.

You can prepare an attack (F)
You can move an obstacle away from you (M)
You can destroy the obstacle (O)

Enemies can attack you and eventually this will kill you.

Your first move will be a double move.
